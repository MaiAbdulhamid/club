al azhar science club

ASC is a non-profitable organisation established in may 5,2013, by 6 students at faculty of engineering, Al-azhar University to share the pation of science with every student in Egypt, through our different projects such as providing technical courses, scientific magazine and simplifing scientific concepts on our facebook page.

what we do

Al Azhar science club crew proudly produced and worked main projects EXERGY, Magazine and facebook page.

EXERGY is the first event of it's kind to take place in faculty of engineering, Al Azhar university, the first EXERGY event was in 2014 with around 1450 applications in the year, and about 350 direct benifications.
